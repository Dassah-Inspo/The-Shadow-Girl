FINDING ME

-For The Girl Behind The Light

I didn't know until I was lost, until the world grew quiet-until the noise outside faded into the hush of my own breath. Behind these bars not of prison, but of pause,I met the parts of me no mirror had ever shown. The sun touched my skin like a wispher: you are here. Not in the clutter of the day , not in the names they gave me, but in the silence I once feared to sit with. My hand held my face not in wariness-but in wonder. As if to ask gently, Who are you when no one is watching? I closed my eyes and opened a window within me. There I saw a girl-not perfect, but present, not loud but luminous. And she smiled at me from the quiet corner of my heart, where sun light meets the soul and says: Welcome Back.
